About The Matrix

Info about The Matrix: A movie about our main character Neo who lives a normal life with an office job in new york. That later meets a man named Morpheus who claims the world he lives in is a lie and all a simulation. He gives him the choice, to continue living the lie or go with him to the real world Neo chooses the real world later finding out he is the "one" and fights the AI robots to for the sake of humanity.
